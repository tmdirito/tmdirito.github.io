What makes a hero?

A hero is someone that we look up to. Heroes embody what we truly value most. Therefore who we select as role models says a lot about who we are as people.

J. Cole is someone who stands out amongst their craft. He is a man who helps others, uplifts others, and spreads positivity to the world. Cole gives his childhood home to struggling single mothers to stay in for free, and he encourages those who are doing what they are passionate about to keep pursuing.
